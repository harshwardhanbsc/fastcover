STI's are common in Bali

It's important to pratise safe sex so that you iminimise your risk of STI's and Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV). Sexually trasmitted infections (STI's) are common when travelling. Bring protection (Condoms) with you which meets international safety standards. STI's are generally not covered by travel insurance.
