Dont risk your life for drugs!

Indonesia has harsh laws when it comes to drugs. If you plan on buying, selling or taking drugs in Bali, be aware! Visitors to indonesia are greetedy by a sign saying's Death to Drugn Traffickers's and this sentiment extends to drug users as well.

Corrupt traffic police are constantly on the lookout for tourists driving motorbikes or cars.

  • If you want to drive, familiarise yourself with all the rules. Obey them. See Zen Driving Rules for more information.
  • Wear a helmet!
  • Expect to be stopped by police.
  • Carry you international driver's licence with you when driving.
  • If you're cought by corrupt police you'll probably lose out financially.
  • Bribing is illegal in Bali so try to avoid the police where possible.
