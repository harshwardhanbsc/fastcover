Eat clean to avoid disasters

Enjoy the food in Bali but carefully select the restaurants where you choose to eat. Some restaurants or street food stalls lack basic hygiene standards and many not wash their hands, plates or utensils properly

  • Choose restaurants or street stalls with a high turnover and lots of locals.
  • Don't eat food that has been standing all day as it may be contaminated. Choose food that is cooked to order only.
  • Ask for meat and fish to be well done.
  • Good restaurants are well known amongst tourists. Ask around for advice or ask you hotel for recommendatioons.
  • Don't over indulge in spicy food or tropical fruit.
  • Don't drink local water!
